KOREA

South Korea is a dynamic mix of ancient tradition and modern, high-tech culture, featuring attractions like Seoul’s Gyeongbokgung Palace,
the scenic, volcanic Jeju Island, and the bustling beaches of Busan.

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace (Seoul): Best visited September–November; famous for historical tours and changing guard ceremonies.
  • Cherry Blossoms (Nationwide): Early April is the peak time, especially in Seoul and southern regions.
  • Jeju Island (Spring/Summer): Renowned for nature, beaches, and hiking.
